# Official School Management App - Comprehensive Credential Fields

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive credential and data fields required for a production-ready school management application (SchoolSync). These fields ensure compliance with educational standards, data privacy regulations, and operational requirements.

---

## 1. STUDENT CREDENTIALS

### Personal Information
- **Full Name** (First, Middle, Last)
- **Date of Birth** (with age calculation)
- **Gender** (Male/Female/Other/Prefer not to say)
- **Blood Group** (for medical emergencies)
- **Nationality/Citizenship**
- **Religion** (optional, based on region)
- **Mother Tongue**
- **Profile Photo** (with consent)

### Contact Information
- **Personal Email** (if applicable)
- **Phone Number** (primary)
- **Emergency Contact Number**
- **Residential Address** (full address with pincode/zip)
- **City/State/Country**
- **Permanent Address** (if different from residential)

### Academic Information
- **Student ID / Roll Number** (unique identifier)
- **Admission Number** (system-generated)
- **Admission Date**
- **Current Class/Grade**
- **Section/Division**
- **Academic Year**
- **Previous School Details** (name, address, transfer certificate)
- **Academic Session** (current academic year)

### Family/Guardian Information
- **Father's Name**
- **Father's Occupation**
- **Father's Phone Number**
- **Father's Email**
- **Father's Education Qualification**
- **Mother's Name**
- **Mother's Occupation**
- **Mother's Phone Number**
- **Mother's Email**
- **Mother's Education Qualification**
- **Guardian Name** (if different from parents)
- **Guardian Relationship**
- **Guardian Contact Details**
- **Annual Family Income** (for scholarship eligibility)

### Medical Information
- **Allergies** (food, medication, environmental)
- **Chronic Health Conditions**
- **Blood Pressure** (if applicable)
- **Vision/Hearing Impairments**
- **Physical Disabilities** (with accessibility requirements)
- **Medications** (current prescriptions)
- **Emergency Medical Notes**
- **Insurance Details** (policy number, provider)
- **Vaccination Records** (with dates)

### Academic Performance
- **Previous Class Grades/Marks**
- **GPA/Percentage**
- **Attendance Record** (yearly/monthly)
- **Extracurricular Activities**
- **Achievements/Awards**
- **Behavioral Reports**
- **Special Needs/IEP** (Individualized Education Program)
- **Learning Disabilities** (if any, with documentation)

### Transportation
- **Bus Route Number** (if applicable)
- **Bus Stop Details**
- **Pick-up/Drop-off Points**
- **Transport Fee Status**

### Financial Information
- **Fee Structure Applied**
- **Fee Payment Status**
- **Scholarship Details** (if any)
- **Fee Concession Category**
- **Outstanding Dues**
- **Payment History**

### System/Security
- **Username/Login ID**
- **Password** (encrypted)
- **Security Questions** (for password recovery)
- **Two-Factor Authentication Status**
- **Last Login Date/Time**
- **Account Status** (Active/Inactive/Suspended)
- **Device Registration** (for multi-device access)

## 10. LEGAL & DISCLOSURE

### Important Notes
1. **Data Privacy**: All personal data must be encrypted at rest and in transit
2. **Consent Management**: Explicit consent required for data processing
3. **Right to Access**: Users must be able to access their data
4. **Right to Deletion**: Users must be able to request data deletion
5. **Data Retention**: Define retention periods for different data types
6. **Audit Trail**: Maintain comprehensive logs for all data changes
7. **Backup & Recovery**: Regular backups with disaster recovery plan
8. **Access Control**: Role-based access control (RBAC) implementation
9. **Compliance**: Ensure compliance with local educational regulations
10. **Security**: Regular security audits and penetration testing
