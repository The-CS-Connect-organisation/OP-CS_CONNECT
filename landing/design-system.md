# SchoolSync Design System — Cornerstone International

## Product Type
**School Management SaaS** — Enterprise-grade unified platform for students, teachers, parents, and administrators.

## Target Audience
- School administrators making purchasing decisions
- Teachers who need efficient workflows
- Parents tracking their children's progress
- Students accessing coursework and grades

## Style Direction
**Modern Institutional** — Professional credibility meets digital innovation. Clean, authoritative, trustworthy.

## Color Palette
```
Primary:    #FF6B35 (warm orange — action, energy, education)
Secondary:  #1A1A2E (deep navy — authority, trust, institutional)
Accent:     #00D4AA (teal — innovation, success, technology)
Background: #FAFBFC (off-white — clean, spacious)
Surface:    #FFFFFF (white cards/surfaces)
Text Primary:   #0F172A (near-black)
Text Secondary: #64748B (muted gray)
```

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter (700, 800, 900) — clean, modern, highly legible
- **Body**: Inter (400, 500) — consistent, professional
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono for data/code elements

## Layout System
- 8px spacing base
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)

## Motion Philosophy
- Subtle entrance animations (fade + translate up)
- Scroll-triggered reveals with staggered timing
- Micro-interactions on buttons/links (150-250ms)
- NO playful bouncing or emoji animations

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
❌ Emojis as structural icons
❌ Decorative orbs/blobs
❌ Gradient text for long copy
❌ Overly playful "glassmorphism"
❌ Hardcoded fake stats (10K+ students)
❌ Generic "ONE PLATFORM" messaging
